Quality Assurance

We ensure complete oversight & control over incoming materials, in-process material and the final inspection of each batch. Identification of each batch is maintained and test certificates against each supply is issued to customers. We have quality approval from reputed testing laboratories.

Our plant is equipped with excellent manufacturing and testing facilities in order to ensure that our products conform to the Indian and International standards. We are in the process of acquiring ISO 9001:2008 certification.
