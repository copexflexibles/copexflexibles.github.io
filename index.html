About Us

Our range of products consists of Copper Flexibles, Braids, Flexible Connectors, in Bare, Tin, Silver Plated catering to Electrical, Electronics, Automobile and Allied Industries.

Our products are manufactured inhouse to ensure highest grade of quality & service quality at competitive prices. Each product we manufacture confirms to BIS/DIN standards.

We have upwards of 3 decades of experience and are an able, willing, sincere and dedicated organisation committed to customer's satisfaction. We are capable of developing customized products according to the customer’s requirement

Quality Assurance

We ensure complete oversight & control over incoming materials, in-process material and the final inspection of each batch. Identification of each batch is maintained and test certificates against each supply is issued to customers. We have quality approval from reputed testing laboratories.

Our plant is equipped with excellent manufacturing and testing facilities in order to ensure that our products conform to the Indian and International standards. We are in the process of acquiring ISO 9001:2008 certification.
